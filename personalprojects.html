If we had more time we would add different loop types (like an infinity symbol, square, etc), different body parts to control, different characters and environments to choose from, more songs, and maybe throw in some unexpected challenges.

Technical Summary:

The most challenging part of this project was determining how to keep score. I wanted to test how far the mouse was from the desired shape at any given point, make sure they were going in the right direction, and make sure they completed the shape. It was difficult figuring out how to determine the distance from the shape since I didn't know what part of the shape they were currently at. I decided to go with a distance formula from the mouse's current position to the closest point on a circle. I was hoping to find an equation that would work for different shapes as well, but didn't have the time. Since every shape should be a closed loop, I also check what percentage of a loop they completed. This way I could also check if they were moving backwards and subtract points accordingly.
